About. I created this page first for myself to understand the details of UWB PHY layer. My interest in engineering started by this curiosity to understand, preferably visually, to the atomic level. I have worked with UWB based positioning systems for the last couple of years, but always had a nagging feeling that I do not have a clear picture of all the components of UWB PHY layer. Alas, I did not find any resource which provided an insightful way to understand the UWB PHY layer. I decided to build it myself. This page was created using Claude Code.
